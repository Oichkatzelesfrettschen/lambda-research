# Calculus of Constructions - Academic Bibliography

## Foundational Works

### 1. Coquand, Thierry (1985)
**"Une Théorie des Constructions"**
- PhD Thesis, Université Paris VII, January 1985
- Original introduction of the Calculus of Constructions
- Foundational work establishing dependent types with impredicativity

### 2. Coquand, Thierry & Huet, Gérard (1988)
**"The Calculus of Constructions"**
- Information and Computation 76(2-3): 95-120
- Definitive journal publication of the Calculus of Constructions
- Complete formal development and meta-theory

### 3. Coquand, Thierry & Huet, Gérard (1986)
**"The Calculus of Constructions"**
- Technical Report RR-0530, INRIA, May 1986
- Earlier technical report version of the foundational work

### 4. Coquand, Thierry & Huet, Gérard (1985)
**"Constructions: A Higher Order Proof System for Mechanizing Mathematics"**
- European Conference on Computer Algebra: 151-184
- Springer-Verlag, Berlin Heidelberg
- First presentation of the system

### 5. Coquand, Thierry (1989)
**"Metamathematical Investigations of a Calculus of Constructions"**
- Technical Report RR-1088, INRIA, September 1989
- Meta-theoretical analysis and properties

## Lambda Cube and Pure Type Systems

### 6. Barendregt, Henk P. (1991)
**"Introduction to Generalized Type Systems"**
- Journal of Functional Programming 1(2): 125-154
- Introduction of the lambda cube framework
- Positioning Calculus of Constructions within the cube

### 7. Barendregt, Henk P. (1992)
**"Lambda Calculi with Types"**
- In: Handbook of Logic in Computer Science, Volume II
- Oxford University Press
- Comprehensive treatment including the lambda cube

### 8. Berardi, Stefano (1988)
**"Towards a Mathematical Analysis of the Coquand-Huet Calculus of Constructions and Other Systems in Barendregt's Cube"**
- Technical Report CMU-CS-88-131, Carnegie Mellon University
- Early work on pure type systems

### 9. Terlouw, Jan (1989)
**"Een nadere bewijstheoretische analyse van GSTTs"**
- Manuscript, University of Nijmegen
- Contributions to pure type systems theory

### 10. Geuvers, Herman (1993)
**"Logics and Type Systems"**
- PhD Thesis, University of Nijmegen
- Comprehensive analysis of pure type systems

## Paradoxes and Consistency

### 11. Coquand, Thierry (1986)
**"An Analysis of Girard's Paradox"**
- Symposium on Logic in Computer Science
- IEEE Computer Society Press, Cambridge, MA
- Analysis of consistency issues

### 12. Hurkens, Antonius J.C. (1995)
**"A Simplification of Girard's Paradox"**
- Typed Lambda Calculi and Applications: 266-278
- Springer-Verlag, Lecture Notes in Computer Science 902
- Simplified presentation of the paradox

### 13. Girard, Jean-Yves (1972)
**"Interprétation fonctionnelle et élimination des coupures de l'arithmétique d'ordre supérieur"**
- PhD Thesis, Université Paris VII
- Original work containing the paradox

### 14. Burali-Forti, Cesare (1897)
**"Una questione sui numeri transfiniti"**
- Rendiconti del Circolo Matematico di Palermo 11: 154-164
- Classical paradox influencing type theory

### 15. Lof, Per Martin- (1971)
**"A Theory of Types"**
- Manuscript, University of Stockholm
- Early dependent type theory with consistency issues
