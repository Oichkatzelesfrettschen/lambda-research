## Historical Foundations (Pre-1980)

### Lambda Calculus Origins

**Church, Alonzo (1936)**
*An Unsolvable Problem of Elementary Number Theory*
**Access**: Open Access | **Type**: Core, Historical
**Path**: `historical/church-lambda-calculus/1936_church_unsolvable_problem_oa.pdf`
**URL**: https://ics.uci.edu/~lopes/teaching/inf212W12/readings/church.pdf
**Significance**: Introduces lambda calculus and proves undecidability of the Entscheidungsproblem

**Church, Alonzo (1941)**
*The Calculi of Lambda-Conversion*
**Access**: Public Domain | **Type**: Core, Historical
**Path**: `historical/church-lambda-calculus/1941_church_calculi_lambda_conversion_pd.pdf`
**URL**: https://archive.org/details/AnnalsOfMathematicalStudies6ChurchAlonzoTheCalculiOfLambdaConversionPrincetonUniversityPress1941
**Significance**: Comprehensive treatment of lambda calculus after resolving consistency issues

### Combinatory Logic

**Curry, Haskell B.; Feys, Robert (1958)**
*Combinatory Logic, Volume I*
**Access**: Institutional Repository | **Type**: Core, Historical
**Path**: `historical/curry-combinatory-logic/1958_curry_feys_combinatory_logic_ir.pdf`
**Significance**: Foundational work on combinatory logic as alternative to lambda calculus

### Domain Theory and Denotational Semantics

**Scott, Dana S. (1970)**
*Outline of a Mathematical Theory of Computation*
**Access**: Open Access | **Type**: Core, Historical
**Path**: `historical/scott-domain-theory/1970_scott_mathematical_theory_computation_oa.pdf`
**Significance**: Introduces domain theory for lambda calculus semantics

**Scott, Dana S.; Strachey, Christopher (1971)**
*Toward a Mathematical Semantics for Computer Languages*
**Access**: Open Access | **Type**: Core, Historical
**Path**: `historical/scott-domain-theory/1971_scott_strachey_mathematical_semantics_oa.pdf`
**Significance**: Establishes denotational semantics methodology
