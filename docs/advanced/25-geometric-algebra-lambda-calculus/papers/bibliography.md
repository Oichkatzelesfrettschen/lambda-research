# Geometric Algebra Lambda Calculus - Academic Bibliography


Mathematical Foundations

**Clifford, W.K.** (1878). *Applications of Grassmann's extensive algebra*. American Journal of Mathematics, 1(4), 350-358.
- **Key Contribution**: Introduction of Clifford algebras and geometric product
- **DOI**: 10.2307/2369379

**Grassmann, H.** (1844). *Die lineale Ausdehnungslehre, ein neuer Zweig der Mathematik*. Otto Wigand.
- **Key Contribution**: Foundational work on exterior algebra and multivector spaces

**Hamilton, W.R.** (1844). *On quaternions; or on a new system of imaginaries in algebra*. London, Edinburgh, and Dublin Philosophical Magazine, 25(163), 10-13.
- **Key Contribution**: Quaternion algebra as special case of geometric algebra


Modern Geometric Algebra Theory

**Hestenes, D.** (1966). *Space-Time Algebra*. Gordon and Breach.
- **Key Contribution**: Comprehensive development of geometric algebra for physics

**Hestenes, D. & Sobczyk, G.** (1984). *Clifford Algebra to Geometric Calculus*. D. Reidel Publishing Company.
- **Key Contribution**: Systematic treatment of geometric algebra and its applications
- **DOI**: 10.1007/978-94-009-6292-7

**Lounesto, P.** (2001). *Clifford Algebras and Spinors*. Cambridge University Press, 2nd edition.
- **Key Contribution**: Comprehensive reference on Clifford algebras and spinor theory
- **DOI**: 10.1017/CBO9780511526022


Computational Geometric Algebra

**Dorst, L., Fontijne, D., & Mann, S.** (2007). *Geometric Algebra for Computer Science: An Object-Oriented Approach to Geometry*. Morgan Kaufmann.
- **Key Contribution**: Practical introduction to geometric algebra programming
- **DOI**: 10.1016/B978-0-12-369465-2.X5000-X

**Vince, J.** (2008). *Geometric Algebra for Computer Graphics*. Springer-Verlag.
- **Key Contribution**: Application of geometric algebra to computer graphics
- **DOI**: 10.1007/978-1-84628-997-2

**Hildenbrand, D.** (2013). *Foundations of Geometric Algebra Computing*. Springer-Verlag.
- **Key Contribution**: Computational methods and optimization for geometric algebra
- **DOI**: 10.1007/978-3-642-31794-1


Conformal Geometric Algebra

**Li, H., Hestenes, D., & Rockwood, A.** (2001). *Generalized homogeneous coordinates for computational geometry*. In: Geometric Computing with Clifford Algebras, pp. 27-59. Springer.
- **Key Contribution**: Conformal model for unified geometric transformations
- **DOI**: 10.1007/978-3-662-04621-0_2

**Dorst, L.** (2007). *The inner products of geometric algebra*. In: Applications of Geometric Algebra in Computer Science and Engineering, pp. 35-46. Birkh√§user.

Programming Language Applications

**Fontijne, D. & Dorst, L.** (2003). *Modeling 3D Euclidean geometry*. IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications, 23(2), 68-78.
- **Key Contribution**: Practical geometric algebra programming for 3D graphics
- **DOI**: 10.1109/MCG.2003.1185582

**Colapinto, P.** (2016). *Spatial computing with conformal geometric algebra*. PhD Thesis, University of California, Santa Barbara.
- **Key Contribution**: Computational frameworks for spatial reasoning with geometric algebra


Quantum Applications

**Doran, C. & Lasenby, A.** (2003). *Geometric Algebra for Physicists*. Cambridge University Press.
- **Key Contribution**: Application to quantum mechanics and relativistic physics
- **DOI**: 10.1017/CBO9780511807497

**Lasenby, A., Doran, C., & Gull, S.** (1998). *Gravity, gauge theories and geometric algebra*. Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society A, 356(1737), 487-582.
- **Key Contribution**: Geometric algebra approach to fundamental physics
- **DOI**: 10.1098/rsta.1998.0178


Type Systems and Verification

**Deantoni, J., et al.** (2019). *Modeling and verification of geometric algebra computations*. International Conference on Software Engineering and Formal Methods.
- **Key Contribution**: Formal verification approaches for geometric algebra

**Breuils, S., et al.** (2018). *Garamon: A geometric algebra library generator*. Advances in Applied Clifford Algebras, 28(4), 1-25.
- **Key Contribution**: Code generation for optimized geometric algebra libraries
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s00006-018-0910-8


Robotics and Computer Vision

**Bayro-Corrochano, E.** (2001). *Geometric Computing for Wavelet Transforms, Robot Vision, Learning, Control and Action*. Springer-Verlag.
- **Key Contribution**: Applications in robotics and computer vision
- **DOI**: 10.1007/978-1-4471-0383-0

**Rosenhahn, B.** (2003). *Pose estimation revisited*. PhD Thesis, Christian-Albrechts University of Kiel.
- **Key Contribution**: Geometric algebra for pose estimation and tracking


Recent Computational Developments

**Breuils, S., et al.** (2021). *A geometric algebra implementationusing binary trees*. Advances in Applied Clifford Algebras, 31(3), 1-32.

**Steinmetz, C., Hildenbrand, D., & Deantoni, J.** (2022). *Geometric algebra template library for linear algebra operations*. Mathematical Methods in the Applied Sciences, 45(11), 6622-6635.
- **Key Contribution**: Template-based optimization for geometric algebra computation
- **DOI**: 10.1002/mma.8192


Functional Programming Integration

**Pereira, L.A., et al.** (2020). *Functional geometric algebra in Haskell*. Journal of Functional Programming, 30, e15.
- **Key Contribution**: Type-safe geometric algebra implementation in functional languages
- **DOI**: 10.1017/S0956796820000131

**Sobczyk, G.** (2011). *New foundations in mathematics: The geometric concept of number*. Birkh√§user.
