# Comprehensive Bibliography: Substructural Lambda Calculi

### Structural Rules and Their Elimination

- **Weakening**: Allows discarding unused assumptions
- **Contraction**: Allows duplicating assumptions
- **Exchange**: Allows reordering assumptions

Different combinations yield distinct calculi:
- **Linear**: No weakening or contraction (use exactly once)
- **Affine**: No contraction (use at most once)
- **Relevant**: No weakening (use at least once)
- **Ordered**: No exchange (use in order)
